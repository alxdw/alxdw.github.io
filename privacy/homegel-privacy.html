homegel Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

homegel is an endurance fuel calculator and recipe reference app for iOS. It is designed as a local calculator and reference tool for planning homemade endurance fuel.

This policy explains what information homegel processes, what leaves the app, and the limits of the nutrition information shown in the app.

Information Processed On Your Device

homegel does not require an account, login, subscription, or backend service. Calculator inputs, recipe scaling values, and method content are processed locally on your device.

The app may store small local preferences or counters, such as the number of times the app has been opened so it can decide when to request an App Store rating. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the app publisher.

Information Sent To The App Publisher

homegel does not collect calculator inputs, recipe values, usage history, location, device identifiers, or analytics events.

Feedback

If you send feedback by email, your email app sends your message to feedback@w5y.me. Your message may include anything you type, your email address, and basic diagnostic details included in the draft, such as app version, iOS version, and device model. This information is used to respond to feedback and improve the app.

Feedback email is processed by the email providers involved in sending and receiving the message. The app publisher keeps feedback only as long as reasonably needed to respond, troubleshoot, and maintain the app.

Affiliate Links And Third-Party Websites

homegel includes optional affiliate links to third-party websites for ingredients and reusable containers. These links are user-initiated and open outside the app.

If you choose to open one of these links, your browser and the third-party website may receive information about that visit, such as your IP address, browser details, and the link you opened. The third-party website handles that information according to its own privacy policy.

homegel does not use IP geolocation to choose links and does not run background tracking for affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate, the app publisher may earn from qualifying purchases.

App Store And Apple Services

homegel may use Apple's standard App Store review prompt. Apple may process information related to App Store ratings, reviews, purchases, downloads, crash reports, or diagnostics according to Apple's own privacy policies and the settings on your device.

Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

homegel does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

homegel does not sell personal information. The app publisher does not share calculator inputs, recipe values, or local app counters because those are not collected.

Information may be processed by third parties only when you choose to use a feature that involves them, such as sending feedback by email, opening an affiliate link, or interacting with Apple App Store services.

Health And Nutrition Disclaimer

homegel provides practical endurance-fuel calculations, recipes, and method notes for informational purposes only. It is not medical advice, nutrition advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Carbohydrate, electrolyte, hydration, and ingredient targets can vary based on your health, training history, gut tolerance, exercise intensity, weather, event duration, medications, and medical conditions. Use your own judgment, test fueling strategies before important events, and consult a qualified medical or nutrition professional if you have health concerns or dietary restrictions.

Children's Privacy

homegel is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date will change when the policy is revised.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, email feedback@w5y.me.
