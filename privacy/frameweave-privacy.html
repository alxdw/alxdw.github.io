Frameweave Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Frameweave is a photo framing and export app for iOS. The app is designed to process your photos on your device.

Photos

When you choose photos in Frameweave, the app uses them locally so you can frame, preview, and save finished images. Frameweave does not upload your photos to a server.

When you save finished frames, Frameweave asks iOS for permission to add images to your photo library. This permission is used only to save the images you choose to export.

Feedback

If you send feedback by email, your email app sends your message to feedback@w5y.me. Your message may include anything you type, your email address, and basic diagnostic details included in the draft, such as app version, iOS version, and device model. This information is used to respond to feedback and improve the app.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Frameweave does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

Data Sharing

Frameweave does not sell personal information. Feedback email may be processed by the email providers involved in sending and receiving the message.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, email feedback@w5y.me.
